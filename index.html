2D in Retail Guidelines

23 April 2025

Abstract

This document outlines the global retail initiative to migrate from 1D to 2D barcodes by 2027, enabling advanced digital capabilities at the point-of-sale.

Overview

The shift to 2D barcodes brings greater data capacity and flexibility. These codes support digital links, traceability, and enhanced consumer interaction.

Key Benefits

  • Faster and more accurate checkouts
  • Increased product transparency
  • Support for sustainability and traceability
  • Dynamic linking to real-time content
