Abstract

This document describes the GS1 Digital License Ecosystem. GS1 and its country-based member organizations (MO) form a federated system to issue unique identifiers to its Member Companies (MC) for trade items and entities including, but not limited to, GLN, GTIN, GRAI, SSCC, and other GS1 ID keys. As trade communications is increasingly digital, GS1 is extending their license system to provide digital licenses using W3C Verifiable Credentials (VC). These digital licenses allow companies to prove authority over their GS1 ID keys and product data. This document is intended for Member Company IT architects and developers wishing to learn and use GS1 Digital Licenses to issue trusted GS1 ID keys and verifiable product data.
